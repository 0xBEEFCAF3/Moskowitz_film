About Me

I’m Sarah Moskowitz. I was born and raised in Seattle, but I have lived in Newton, MA for a while now. I am studying film at NYU Tisch School of the Arts, and am also pursuing a second major in politics. Recently, I have found myself gravitating towards documentary and television work.
